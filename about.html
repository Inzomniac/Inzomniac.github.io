About this Site

This website has been developed as part of a Level 5 Web Development paper in the BCS at EIT. The purpose of this site is for me, Jarrod, to share the information I collection regarding the History of IT

in each section of the website you will find interatactive timelines for the development of computer components over time. I will also be adding interesting articles to these sections as I come across them, these articles will relate to;
->CPU Development and History
->GPU Development and History
->RAM Development and History
->Storage Development and History

The website was developed by Jarrod McLaren in 2022 as a modern alternitve to a hardware information websites available at the time, the vision for this site is to offer people interested in IT a place to learn about components and how they were developed overtime.

About Me

Hi, I am Jarrod McLaren, as of 2022 I am a student at EIT Taradale.
I am aspiring to be a programmer as this is my strength in IT however along my path I have developed a love for hardware after becoming very interested in how the very powerful machines we have in the modern era came to be I decided I needed a place to explain my findings. I try to learn something about hardware configuration everyday to expand my knowledge and this site will be the perfect place to blog my interest in a fun and active way.
